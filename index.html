    An American Restaurant & Event Venue In Shelbyville


    The finest in country cooking and family-style dining is always the fare when you visit Claudia Sanders Dinner House. Our restaurant carries on the traditions of our founders, Colonel Sanders and his wife, Claudia. Their knowledge of southern recipes and their skill in the restaurant business soon brought about the creation of the Claudia Sanders Dinner House, where an air of Southern Hospitality and cooking combined with old fashioned, family-style service remain. We’re proud to be a local landmark, attracting tourists and visitors from all over.

    DELICIOUS FOOD MADE FROM SCRATCH

    AN EVENT VENUE UNLIKE ANY OTHER

    Our facility offers a wide variety of rooms to host your next event, whether it be a wedding reception, Quinceañera, banquet, family reunion, wedding or baby shower, seminar, business luncheon or any other private party. We have smaller rooms for more intimate events, or you can book the Colonel’s Quarters, our largest banquet room. It holds up to 450 seated guests or standing room of up to 1,000!


    We design our service around your needs for your event and can provide a buffet style, station-style, family-style, plated, or hand passed service. We also have a full beverage service available for all events!


    If you're looking for the perfect place for your country wedding, then we are the ideal spot for you. Not only are our facilities excellent for your reception, but we also offer areas for the actual ceremony, with both our elegant Main Dining Room and outdoor Courtyard Garden Wedding venues.
